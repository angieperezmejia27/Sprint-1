Pink Lilies

If you’re looking for a bloom that will last from mid-summer to the first winter frost, pink lilies are the perfect floral accessory to bring elegance to your home. The lily group contains several varieties, including calla lilies and white lilies. Unlike its finicky sisters, the late-blooming pink lily—with its delicate, pink pastel petals and lovely fragrance—will last a long time with minimal care.

Where to Find

As a part of the Lilium genus, pink lilies grow from bulbs. Placing the bulb deeper in the soil allows for sturdy roots and helps the bulb stay cool when the temperature rises. Adding a layer of mulch can also help keep the bulbs cool, especially if you’re heading into the winter months. Pink lilies are sometimes called Oriental lilies. Well-known Oriental lily cultivars include Star Gazer, Journey’s End, and Mona Lisa in shades of pink and Casa Blanca, which has white flowers.
